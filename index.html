What is the Ergonomy ?

Ergonomics; the physical and psychological assessment of man is a group of naturally
and technically research and development studies of human compatibility
with the machine and the environment.

Why we should do tests ?

Risk analysis is carried out for employers by proving and caused by the dangers existing in the workplace or from outside.
This study is carried out with the participation of employees as well as employers and ISG students.
Keeping risk analyzes up to date ensures continuous improvement in the workplace.
